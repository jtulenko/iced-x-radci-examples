Welcome

GIAMachine is a computational engine (Machine) for visualizing, analyzing, and comparing model outputs from simulations of the gravitational, rotational, and deformational effects of ice and water loading on the solid Earth -- processes together referred to as glacial isostatic adjustment (GIA).
